Materials

  • 2 colors of yarn (I used Adore-A-Ball from Yarn Bee)
  • 8mm safety eyes
  • Crochet hook (I used 4.5mm)
  • Yarn Needle
  • Stuffing
  • Stitch Marker
  • Scissors

Abreviations

  • R = round
  • st = stitch
  • mr = magic ring
  • sc = single crochet
  • inc = increase
  • dec = decrease
  • hdc = half double crochet
  • slst = slip stitch
  • FLO = front loops only
  • BLO = back loops only
  • FO = fasten off

Ice Cream Pattern

~start in ice cream color~

R1: 6sc in mr (6)

R2:6inc (12)

R3:(sc, inc)x6 (18)

R4:(2sc, inc)x6 (24)

R5-R7:sc around (24)

R8:(sc, dec)x8 (16)

place a stitch marker in BLO of next st

R9:FLO(3hdc in one st, slst into next st)x8 (16)

~color change to cone color~

R10:work where stitch market was placed in BLO of R9, sc around (16)

add safety eyes between rounds 6 & 7, with 4 st in between

start stuffing

R11:(6sc, dec)x2 (14)

R12:(5sc, dec)x2 (12)

continue stuffing as we finish

R13:(4sc, dec)x2 (10)

R14:sc around (10)

R15:(3sc, dec)x2 (8)

R16:(2sc, dec)x2 (6)

slst in next st. FO and sew hole closed
