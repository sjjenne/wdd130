Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to display a variety of crochet plushies. The intended use is to promote my crochet business and create easy viewing for customers.

Audience

This site is targetting those interested in learning more about Sammy's Crochet Crafts, and people looking to purchase crochet plushie(s)!

Content

Home page

My web page will attract people that want to learn more about my crochet business, browse my plushies or make a purchase! This can include a few different types of people. Those that want to learn more about my business can be fellow crocheters that are interested in materials, strategies or suggestions I have/use. Those browsing my plushies are those interested in purchasing! They have the ability to learn more about my shop, and build trust in my business before placing an order. They can really get to know my business, me, and feel a more personal connection based on how I design my website.

Images for the Home page

About & FAQ

Users can learn about me, frequently asked questions and other information they might be looking for. I'll list answers to questions such as what my bestseller is, when I started my business, where I get my supplies and etc.

Patterns

Many of my current followers are fellow crocheters, which leads me to believe many of these people will also use my site if I promote it on social media platforms. To maximize the use for those that crochet, I will list the patterns I have created on my website. I only have my patterns listed on my instagram which works, but to create a better experience I could create a page for users to find it on my website.
